Khosrov Reserve

Khosrov Forest State Reserve is located in the historical territory of Khosrov Forest, which was founded in the 4th century as a hunting ground by Armenian king Khosrov B Kotak and in 1958 was registered as “State Reserve”. It is located in Ararat region, on the slopes of the mountain ranges of Geghama, Tasanord, Yeranos, Irits and Khosrovasar mountains, in the basins of the Azat and Vedi rivers. Occupies an area of ​​27,000 hectares. It starts near Dvin, from the bank of the river Araxes, and stretches to the Azat River.
